Cushman & Wakefield

AngularJS, Bootstrap, PostgreSQL


Companies are always looking for new locations to either move or expand. I helped build an application for the Cushman & Wakefield of Minneapolis team. This application allows potential and current customers to see what properties are available.

The goal of Cushman & Wakefield is to inform business owners what office space is available if they are looking to move or expand.



Cushman & Wakefield of Minneapolis asked me and three other developers to help build an application they can use to have easy access to their data. This application is built with AngularJS, Bootstrap, and PostgreSQL. I am currently working on developing more features to the application.

To learn more about what Cushman & Wakefield do visit the site at www.cushmanwakefield.com/en/
